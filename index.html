Who we are

ACM is one of the world's best educational and scientific computing society that delivers resources that advance computing. ACM's professional student chapters aim to provide the wide range of activities for the ACM members and community we live in.

We are a group of Al Yamamah University students who are aspiring to build a community of creative and innovative minds. Our team is made up professional, collaborative and talented individuals. Each of whom is capable to to deliver a unique value to the team, and to inspire for a better change.
